FindMe! Privacy Policy

Effective date: 08 February 2026

1. Who we are

FindMe! is a mobile application that helps users discover nearby people and view the social media profiles and information they choose to share. This Privacy Policy applies only to the FindMe! app.

2. What data we collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address (for login and password reset)
  • Username
  • Authentication data via Firebase Authentication

2.2 Profile Data (Optional)

  • Profile photo
  • Profile rows (apps/services you choose to display)
  • Comment or bio text
  • Social media usernames (e.g., Instagram, Snapchat)

All profile information is voluntarily provided and visible to other users as part of FindMe!'s discovery features.

2.3 Location Data

With your permission, we collect:

  • Approximate GPS coordinates
  • Derived GeoHash values
  • Activity timestamps (e.g., lastUpdated, lastAppclose)

Location data is used solely for nearby discovery functionality. If location services are disabled, nearby features may not function.

2.4 Usage & Device Data

  • Push notification token
  • Device language and app version
  • Crash logs and technical diagnostics

3. How we use your data

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile to nearby users
  • Provide proximity-based discovery
  • Send push notifications
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

4. Legal basis (EU/EEA users)

  • Contract necessity – to provide core features
  • Legitimate interests – security and system integrity
  • Consent – for location services and notifications

5. How we share your data

We do not sell your personal data.

  • Service providers: Firebase and Google Cloud (hosting, authentication, notifications)
  • Other users: Only profile data you choose to make visible

6. Data storage & retention

Account and profile data are stored as long as your account remains active. When you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed from our active systems.

Location and presence data are retained for a limited operational period (e.g., hours or days) to ensure accurate nearby discovery. Outdated location records are automatically removed or overwritten.

Certain technical logs or encrypted backups may be retained for a limited time for security, fraud prevention, and system integrity purposes. Such data is not used for marketing or profiling.

7. Account deletion

You may permanently delete your account at any time within the app via the "Delete Account" option in Settings.

When you delete your account:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account is permanently deleted
  • Your profile data and stored content are removed
  • Your location records are deleted
  • Your profile images stored under your user directory are removed

8. International data transfers

FindMe! uses Firebase and Google Cloud infrastructure. Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States and other regions where our service providers operate.

Appropriate safeguards are applied in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Your data protection rights (EU/EEA)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Right to lodge a complaint

You may exercise these rights by contacting us.

10. Visibility & active status

You may control whether you are visible to other users. If you set your status to inactive or log out, you will not appear in nearby discovery results.

11. Children’s privacy

FindMe! is not intended for children under the minimum legal age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date will be updated accordingly.

13. Contact information

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
